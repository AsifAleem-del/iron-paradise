About Iron Paradise

Established in 2010, Iron Paradise has been the premier fitness destination for athletes, bodybuilders, and fitness enthusiasts of all levels. Our mission is to provide a motivating environment where members can push their limits and achieve extraordinary results.

With over 10,000 square feet of training space, we offer the latest equipment, diverse class offerings, and personalized training programs to help you reach your fitness goals.
